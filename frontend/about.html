About Us

Ride N Serve is a comprehensive urban mobility solution offered through a user-friendly app.

Ride N Serve is a comprehensive urban mobility solution built around one goal: making everyday travel simpler, safer, and more affordable for the people of Rawalpindi and beyond.

Through our app, riders can hail a car instantly, share a ride with others heading the same way, or subscribe to a monthly pick-and-drop plan for a fixed daily commute. Every driver is verified, every trip is monitored, and every fare is transparent from the moment you book.

We're a local team building a service we'd want to use ourselves - which is why safety features like live camera monitoring and the panic button are built into every ride, not sold as an add-on.
